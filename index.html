Who We Are

The Jewish Guardians is an independent watchdog organization dedicated to community safety within Jewish communities. We aggregate information from government-verified sex offender registries and public court records to warn communities about convicted predators operating in their midst.

Our Sources

Every individual listed on our Wall of Shame is sourced exclusively from official, government-verified databases. These include the National Sex Offender Public Website (NSOPW), state-level sex offender registries, public court records, and law enforcement arrest reports. We do not publish unverified allegations or unsubstantiated claims.

Why This Matters

In tight-knit communities, predators exploit trust, institutional loyalty, and cultural taboos around discussing abuse. Many registered sex offenders relocate or embed themselves in communities where their status is unknown. We bridge that information gap — making publicly available registry data accessible and contextualized for Jewish communities.

Our Process

→

We identify registered sex offenders who are active members of, or operate within, Jewish communities.

→

We cross-reference their status against official government registries and court records.

→

We verify current location, community affiliation, and any relevant institutional connections.

→

Only individuals confirmed through government-verified sources are listed on our Wall of Shame.

Legal Disclaimer

All information published on this website is sourced from publicly available government records, including sex offender registries, court filings, and arrest reports. The Jewish Guardians does not operate as a court of law. We strongly encourage all victims to report abuse directly to law enforcement. If a child is in immediate danger, call 911.
